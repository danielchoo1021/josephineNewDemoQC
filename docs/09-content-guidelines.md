# Le_Almmora Content Guidelines

Version: 1.0

---

# Objective

This document defines the content strategy, writing style, and messaging guidelines for the Le_Almmora website.

All content should maintain a luxurious, elegant, trustworthy, and premium brand image.

---

# Brand Personality

Le_Almmora is a luxury travel and lifestyle brand.

Every sentence should communicate:

- Luxury
- Exclusivity
- Relaxation
- Trust
- Premium Experience
- Emotional Connection

Avoid sounding like a discount travel agency.

---

# Brand Voice

Tone

- Elegant
- Sophisticated
- Warm
- Inspiring
- Professional
- Minimal

Avoid

- Aggressive selling
- Clickbait
- Cheap promotional language
- Excessive emojis
- ALL CAPS

---

# Writing Style

Use short paragraphs.

Use meaningful headings.

Keep sentences concise.

Focus on benefits instead of features.

Write naturally.

Avoid unnecessary technical language.

# SEO Writing

Use natural keywords.

Avoid keyword stuffing.

Every page must include:

- One H1
- Logical H2 hierarchy
- Meaningful meta description
- Descriptive image alt text

---

# Localization

Primary Language

English

Future Support

Bahasa Malaysia

Chinese

Japanese

Do not hardcode text inside Blade templates.

Use translation files where possible.

---

# Accessibility

Simple language.

Readable font sizes.

Avoid long paragraphs.

Avoid decorative text that reduces readability.

---

# AI Content Rules

When generating content:

- Follow the luxury brand tone.
- Keep messaging consistent.
- Avoid repeating phrases.
- Avoid generic AI wording.
- Prioritize originality.
- Match the visual design.
- Maintain emotional storytelling.

---

# Final Rule

Every sentence should strengthen the perception of Le_Almmora as a premium luxury destination brand.
